Introduction

My Little Bunny is an action platformer game where you take the role of a brave little bunny trying to protect her galaxy against evil energy-sucking seed.

Each level is composed of a certain number of rounds and each round a set number of enemies spawn.
The enemies will fly to a randomly selected planet and start stealing its energy. It is up to you to kick off the enemies before they destroy too many planets. To help you with this quest you can use your trusty spacecraft to fly between space stations and use your mysterious powers to clear out planets at a time thanks to your mighty strength.
